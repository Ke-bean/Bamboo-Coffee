Bamboo Coffee is a coffeehouse chain based in Kigali, Rwanda. It has currently four coffeehouses in Kigali, the first one at Union Trade Centre (UTC) in Kiyovu, the second one at the MTN centre in Nyarutarama, the third in Kigali City Tower, and the fourth one at Kigali International Airport.
